Orange React
Developer Conference
2022

This year, the conference will last three days, during which more than 20 talks will take place. The expected number of participants is over 10k people from over 100 countries worldwide. This event is a chance to talk with the greatest experts in React.js community. It is packed with interesting lectures, Q/A sessions, exciting talks, and also amazing fun during networking time. Attending it allows you to become a part of a community conference that provides unique content about the innovative tools and techniques in JavaScript. When buying a ticket, you will get some perks and benefits such as: panel discussions, chat with speakers, advice lounges, Q&A sessions, workshops, games room, online afterparty.

Please contact us per Email for any further questions about Orange React Developer Conference 2022!
